Coffee Shop

Our café opened in South Jakarta in August 2018. Located in the Big building, both the building and the cafe space itself were designed by renowned local architects.

The cafe is open to the lobby, features a marble bar, gorgeous green tile, and plenty of seating both in the café and in the comfortable lobby. Drink a coffee out of Japanese porcelain while sitting in the generous leather chairs and listening to the playlist curated by local radio station.
